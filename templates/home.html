About Us

Empower You is a semi-online platform aimed at providing opporutunities to the members of the unordered sector of employment. These include peopple that donot have any type of security and job benefits. They are often the most exploited interms of uneven wage payments, attendance counts and even non-registration as employees. In many cases they are looked down upon and are kept in the worst of living situations and life style. They are often the primal target of diseases and pandemics.

We at Empower You aim to organise this sector by collecting data and information about people who are in need of services and who are in need of Jobs. There must be a clean mention of the information and complete transparency. Since we are dealing with educationally backward class it is our responsibility to get the people around us who belong to the above mentioned catagories connected to our website in order to obtain full benefit of employment schemes

You can play your role and contribute to the lives of the downtrodden by registering thier details on This link

If you are a company member or a contractor lookling for labour then you can register your requirements Here

We also help to connect people to required services by our Listing facility. This will provide a steady source of income for small scale service providers such as potters, caretakers etc,. It will provide the necessry services to the required people in the locality

Our website is a one stop go for all things informations starting form the Precautions that the workers must take to stay healthy to the Latest Pandemic News, Latest Pandemic Statistics and the Most important schemes by the Government of India for the unorganised sector
